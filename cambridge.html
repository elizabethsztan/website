Cambridge recs

If it is not obvious already, I mean Cambridge UK

Eats

  • Flux coffee (best coffee in Cambridge; best coffee I've ever had)
  • Maison Clement Bakery (specifically for bread - the multicereal bread is the best; the coffee is not as good as flux)
  • Monroe Street Bakery (sells out quickly - but best brownie I've ever had)
  • Jack's Gelato (obviously)
  • Hot Numbers (scrambled eggs)
  • Fancett's (for fancy dinner)

Workspaces

  • Waterstones Cafe
  • St. Johns College Cafe
  • University Library
  • West Hub

Things to Do

There is not many things to do in Cambridge, honestly. Besides working. Here are some things to do that aren't work.

  • Go punting
  • Walk along the river from town (start near Jesus Green) to Fen Ditton and back (around 8km?)
  • Walk from Newnham to Grantchester
  • Look at the Fitzwilliam Museum
